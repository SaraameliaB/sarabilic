## Overview

This project marks the creation of my first professional digital portfolio. The goal was to build a clean and accessible online space where I can introduce myself as a young cybersecurity professional, present my background, and share the projects and experiences that shape my growth.

Rather than creating a complex website from scratch, I chose a modern portfolio template and customized it to reflect my own profile, interests, and career direction. The result is a kickstarter portfolio that can grow with me as I gain more experience, work on new projects, and continue developing my technical and GRC knowledge.

## Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a professional starting point for my online presence. As a recent Cybersecurity & Computer Science graduate and Junior Cybersecurity GRC Consultant, I wanted a place that brings together my CV, experience, projects, and personal interests in cybersecurity.

The portfolio is designed to support my growth as a young professional by making it easier to share who I am, what I do, and what I am learning along the way.

## Key Features

- **Personal Introduction**: A clear homepage that introduces who I am, my current role, and my focus within cybersecurity.
- **Experience and CV**: A dedicated space to present my education, professional experience, and downloadable CV.
- **Project Showcase**: A section for highlighting cybersecurity, GRC, and technical projects in a more structured and visual way.
- **Blog Area**: A place to write about cybersecurity topics, personal learnings, portfolio updates, and hands-on projects.
- **Scalable Structure**: A flexible setup that can be expanded over time as my career, projects, and skills continue to develop.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: Used as the main framework for building the portfolio website.
- **Vercel**: Used to deploy and host the website with a simple and accessible production workflow.
- **MDX**: Used to write project pages and blog-style content in a clean, structured format.
- **GitHub**: Used for version control and managing updates to the website.
- **Magic Portfolio Template**: Used as the starting point for the design and structure of the portfolio.

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the main challenges was deciding how to present myself professionally while still keeping the website personal and approachable. I wanted the portfolio to feel polished, but not overly formal or generic.

Through this process, I learned more about customizing a modern Next.js portfolio, working with MDX content, deploying through Vercel, and structuring a website around a clear professional identity. It also helped me think more intentionally about how I want to communicate my cybersecurity background, GRC focus, and technical interests.

## Outcome

The result is a clean starter portfolio that gives me a professional online presence without needing a custom domain or a complex setup. It allows visitors to quickly learn who I am, explore my background, view my CV, and follow future projects or blog posts.

This project is an important first step in building my personal brand as a young cybersecurity professional. It gives me a space that can evolve alongside my career, from my early consulting experience to future technical projects, certifications, and cybersecurity writing.
