## Overview

Campus Stack is a student-driven community at HOWEST focused on bringing students together through technology, collaboration, and shared experiences.

As part of the Campus Stack team, I contribute as an **Event Manager**, where I help organize and coordinate activities that strengthen the community both socially and professionally.

The goal of Campus Stack is not only to create enjoyable student events, but also to build an environment where students can connect, collaborate, and grow outside of the classroom.

## My Role

Within Campus Stack, I help organize and support a variety of events and initiatives, including:

- Movie nights
- LAN parties
- Community outings
- Cybersecurity-related events
- Student engagement activities

I also act as a go-to contact point for students within the community, helping answer questions and supporting newcomers who want to get involved.

![Cybernight Poster](/images/projects/campus-stack/coverpage-02.jpg)

## Learning Through Organizing

One of the most rewarding aspects of being involved in Campus Stack is learning how much work happens behind the scenes of community events.

From planning logistics and coordinating communication to managing expectations and creating welcoming environments, organizing events has taught me valuable skills in:

- Team collaboration
- Communication
- Coordination
- Leadership
- Community building

It has also shown me how important strong student communities are in creating a positive and motivating campus environment.

## Building Experiences That Matter

Whether it’s a small movie night or a larger LAN event, every activity contributes to creating moments where students can relax, meet new people, and share common interests.

Campus Stack has given me the opportunity to contribute to something bigger than individual events: a welcoming student culture centered around technology, creativity, and connection.

## Final Thoughts

Being part of Campus Stack has been an incredibly rewarding experience so far.

It combines organization, teamwork, creativity, and community engagement in a way that perfectly complements my technical and professional growth. I’m excited to continue helping build future events and contribute to the growth of the Campus Stack community at HOWEST.
