## Overview

At the beginning of May, I wrapped up my 3-month internship at **EY Belgium** in Diegem, an experience that turned out to be both professionally and personally rewarding.

During these past months, I had the opportunity to dive deeper into the **GRC domain of cybersecurity** while contributing to an AI-related project within the **FSO Cybersecurity team**.

Beyond technical knowledge, the internship gave me valuable insight into the importance of communication, collaboration, initiative, and continuous learning within a professional environment.

## Exploring GRC & Cybersecurity

One of the most interesting aspects of the internship was experiencing how cybersecurity extends far beyond technical implementation alone.

Working within a GRC-oriented environment exposed me to:

- Cyber risk management
- Governance and compliance processes
- Business-focused cybersecurity discussions
- AI-related cybersecurity considerations
- Stakeholder communication and collaboration

This perspective strongly aligned with my own interest in bridging technical cybersecurity knowledge with strategic decision-making and risk awareness.

![The Head Office](/images/projects/ey-internship/workspace.jpeg)

## Learning Beyond the Technical Side

While the internship helped strengthen my cybersecurity knowledge, one of the biggest takeaways was learning how important soft skills are within consulting and professional environments.

I learned how valuable it is to:

- Ask questions proactively
- Take initiative
- Communicate clearly
- Collaborate effectively
- Stay curious and adaptable

Especially as an intern, I realized that growth often comes from actively stepping outside your comfort zone and making the most of every opportunity to contribute and learn.

## The Team Experience

What made the experience even more enjoyable was the welcoming atmosphere within the team.

From inspiring coffee chats and lunch walks to after-work padel sessions, the internship offered much more than professional learning alone. It was also an opportunity to meet passionate people and experience the collaborative culture within the FSO Cybersecurity team.

I’m incredibly grateful for the guidance, mentorship, and support I received throughout the internship.

A special thank you to:

- Fjodor Belet
- Jonnick Fonke
- The entire EY FSO Cybersecurity team

for creating such a motivating and supportive environment.

## Final Thoughts

This internship reinforced my passion for cybersecurity, GRC, and cyber risk management while giving me valuable real-world experience within a professional consulting environment.

I’m very grateful for everything I learned during these past months and for all the people I had the chance to work with along the way.

Looking forward to the next milestones ahead.
