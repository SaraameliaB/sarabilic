## Overview

Our very first **Campus Stack LAN Party** is officially a wrap. It turned out to be an incredible experience.

The event brought students together for a full day of gaming, technology, and community in a fun and energetic atmosphere.

## Building Community Through Events

Beyond gaming itself, the LAN party was about creating a space where students could connect, relax, and share common interests outside of lectures and coursework.

Organizing events like this requires a huge amount of teamwork and coordination behind the scenes, which made seeing the final result even more rewarding.

![Campus Stack LAN party](/images/projects/campus-stack/coverpage-06.jpg)

## A Huge Team Effort

This event would not have been possible without the incredible effort from the Campus Stack team, participants, and partners who helped bring everything together.

A special thank you to our sponsors:

- Red Bull (Jet Import)
- Orange Cyberdefense
- Centre for Cybersecurity Belgium

Their support helped make the event possible and contributed to creating an amazing experience for everyone involved.

## Final Thoughts

The success of this first LAN party showed how strong and enthusiastic the student community around Campus Stack has become.

We’re already working on future events and ideas, and I’m excited to continue helping build experiences that bring students together through technology and community.
