## Overview

Recently, Campus Stack helped organize and support the **CSCBE qualifier event** on campus.

The event brought together students passionate about cybersecurity and ethical hacking, all eager to tackle technical challenges and test their skills in a competitive environment.

## A Community-Driven Event

It was inspiring to see so many motivated students collaborate, experiment, and search for creative solutions throughout the event.

Cybersecurity competitions like CSCBE create valuable opportunities for students to:

- Strengthen technical skills
- Think creatively under pressure
- Collaborate with peers
- Explore practical cybersecurity challenges

Events like these perfectly reflect the strong cybersecurity culture within the HOWEST community.

## Supporting the Student Community

As part of Campus Stack, it was rewarding to help create an environment where students could focus on learning, competing, and enjoying the experience together.

Seeing so many promising cyber talents challenge themselves and remain engaged throughout the event made the entire experience incredibly worthwhile.

## Final Thoughts

The CSCBE qualifier event once again highlighted how important student initiatives and communities are in helping students grow beyond traditional coursework.

A big thank you to everyone who participated and contributed to making the event a success. Good luck to everyone continuing the challenges!
