## Overview

Recently, I had the opportunity to help organize the very first **Campus Stack Halloween Movie Night** at HOWEST.

This was my first event as part of the Campus Stack team, and it turned out to be an incredibly fun and rewarding experience.

Seeing students come together in such a relaxed and welcoming environment reminded me how important student communities are outside of academics alone.

## A Successful First Event

The event welcomed around 40 students for an evening filled with movies, snacks, and a Halloween atmosphere.

The positive reactions from students made all the preparation completely worth it. Moments like these show how impactful small community initiatives can be in creating a more connected campus experience.

## Learning by Doing

Organizing this event also gave me valuable hands-on experience in:

- Event coordination
- Communication
- Planning logistics
- Team collaboration
- Community engagement

I’m very grateful for the trust and opportunity I received through Campus Stack to contribute to such a welcoming student initiative.

## Final Thoughts

This first event motivated me even more to continue organizing activities for the Campus Stack community.

I’m already excited about the next events we’re planning and looking forward to helping grow a strong and connected student environment at HOWEST.

A huge thank you to HOWEST for supporting the event with food and drinks, and to everyone who joined us for the evening.
